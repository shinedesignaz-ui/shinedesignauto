Why Shine Design for Interiors?

We treat interiors like a detailer’s “surgery room”—measured process, safe chemistry, and the right tools to avoid overwetting or residue.

  • Steam reaches seams/vents without soaking
  • Hot‑water extraction removes embedded soils
  • pH‑appropriate cleaners protect leather & plastics

Our Interior Process

  1. Thorough vacuum, compressed‑air blow‑out, and trash removal.
  2. Plastics, vinyl, and trim cleaned; switches/vents detailed with brushes.
  3. Steam cleaning on high‑touch areas and stains.
  4. Shampoo/extraction on carpets & fabric seats as needed.
  5. Leather cleaned and conditioned with pH‑safe products.
  6. Streak‑free glass; non‑greasy UV interior dressing.

FAQ

How long does it take?

1.5–2.5 hrs for Quick Refresh; 2.5–4 hrs for Deep Clean; up to 5 hrs for Full Interior depending on size & condition.

Can you remove smoke or spill odors?

We tackle source contamination first, then can add ozone treatment. Severe cases may require multiple steps/visits.

Is there an extra charge for heavy pet hair?

Yes—level‑3 pet hair removal is an add‑on due to time and tool wear. We’ll confirm after inspection.
