How to Remove Water Spots Safely

Use chemistry first, abrasion last. Work cool panels. Test on glass before paint.

Book NowCall (480) 528-8227

Quick process

  • Rinse and foam to remove loose grit.
  • Apply dedicated water-spot remover or diluted white vinegar 1:1 for mild deposits.
  • Agitate with soft applicator. Rinse thoroughly.
  • Use light polish only if etching remains, then protect with sealant or coating topper.

What to avoid

Household acids, green pads, and dry scrubbing. These cause micro-marring and halos.
