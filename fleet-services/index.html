Fleet Service FAQs

Common questions about our commercial programs

What's the minimum fleet size?

We offer fleet pricing starting at 5 vehicles. Smaller businesses can still use our services at regular rates with package discounts.

Do you offer payment terms?

Yes! Qualified businesses with 11+ vehicles can apply for NET 30 payment terms. Larger fleets may qualify for custom terms.

Can you service different locations?

Absolutely. We service multiple locations across the Phoenix metro area. Perfect for businesses with distributed fleets.

How much downtime is involved?

Basic wash takes 30-45 minutes per vehicle. Full details take 2-3 hours. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption.

Do you provide service reports?

Yes, all fleet accounts receive detailed monthly reports showing services performed, upcoming schedule, and any issues noted.

What about insurance and liability?

We carry $2M general liability and are fully insured. Certificates of insurance available upon request for all fleet accounts.

Can we customize our service package?

Absolutely! Every fleet is unique. We'll create a custom package that fits your specific needs and budget.

How do we get started?

Call us or fill out our fleet inquiry form. We'll schedule a free consultation to assess your needs and provide a custom quote.
