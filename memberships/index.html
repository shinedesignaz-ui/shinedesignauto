Membership FAQs

Common questions about our membership program

How do I cancel my membership?

Cancel anytime with no penalties. Simply call, email, or use the app. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Can I change plans?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

Do unused services roll over?

Monthly services don't roll over, encouraging regular car care. However, accumulated reward points never expire while membership is active.

Can I share my membership?

Memberships are per vehicle (Elite includes 2). You can add family vehicles at 50% off additional membership cost.

What's the cancellation policy?

No contracts or cancellation fees ever. Cancel anytime and keep using benefits through the end of your paid period.

Are there any hidden fees?

Never! The monthly price is all you pay. No signup fees, no cancellation fees, no surprises. Transparent pricing always.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes, you can pause for up to 3 months per year for travel or other reasons. Your benefits and pricing resume when you return.

Is it really worth it?

Average members save 3-5x their membership cost annually. If you wash monthly and detail quarterly, membership always saves money.
