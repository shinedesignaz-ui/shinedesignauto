Odor Removal FAQs

Common questions about our odor elimination services

How does ozone treatment work?

Ozone (O3) is a powerful oxidizer that breaks down odor molecules at the molecular level. Our commercial generators produce ozone that penetrates every surface and air space in your vehicle.

Is odor removal permanent?

Yes, when the source is properly removed and treated. Our process eliminates odors completely rather than masking them. Results are permanent unless the source is reintroduced.

How long does treatment take?

Basic treatments take 2-3 hours. Professional elimination requires 4-5 hours. Severe cases may need 1-3 days with extended ozone treatment.

Is ozone treatment safe?

Yes, when properly applied. The vehicle is ventilated after treatment, and ozone naturally converts back to oxygen. The car is completely safe to drive afterward.

Can you remove any odor?

We have a 99% success rate. Some extreme cases (like gasoline saturation) may require interior component replacement, which we can coordinate.

Do you guarantee results?

Yes! We offer a 30-day guarantee on professional treatments and 60-day on severe restorations. If the odor returns, we retreat at no charge.

Will it damage my interior?

No. Our methods are safe for all automotive materials including leather, fabric, plastics, and electronics. We use appropriate treatments for each surface.

What about air fresheners?

Air fresheners only mask odors temporarily. Our treatment eliminates the source, so you won't need fresheners. We can add a mild, fresh scent if desired.
