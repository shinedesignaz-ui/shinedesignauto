Frequently asked questions

Do you come to my location?
Yes. We serve the East Valley including Gilbert, Mesa, Chandler, Queen Creek, and San Tan Valley.
How long does a detail take?
Interior or exterior details take 2–4 hours on average. Full details and heavy corrections take longer.
Do you supply water and power?
Yes. Our mobile units are self‑contained. We only need safe access to the vehicle.
Ceramic coating vs PPF?
Coatings add gloss and chemical resistance and reduce wash effort. PPF is a thicker film that guards against chips and abrasions. Many customers use both on high‑impact areas.
Is window tint legal?
We offer multiple shades including AZ‑legal options. See Window Tint for details.
How do I prepare the car?
Remove personal items and child seats if possible. We handle the rest.
What are typical prices?
See Pricing for detailing, coatings, tint, and PPF packages by vehicle size.
What is your cancellation policy?
Reschedule 24 hours in advance when possible. Same‑day cancellations may incur a fee.
