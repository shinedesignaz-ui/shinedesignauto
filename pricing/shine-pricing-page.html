Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our pricing and services

Why do prices vary by vehicle size?

Larger vehicles require more time, products, and effort to detail properly. Our size-based pricing ensures fair rates for all customers while maintaining the same high quality standards.

Are there any hidden fees?

Never! The price you see is the price you pay. We're completely transparent with our pricing. Any additional charges would only apply for excessive conditions discussed before service.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! We offer 10% off for first-time customers, 15% off for monthly maintenance plans, and special rates for fleet accounts. Military and first responders receive 10% off all services.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely! We can create custom packages tailored to your specific needs. Just let us know what you're looking for, and we'll provide a custom quote.

What's your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Same-day cancellations may incur a fee.

Do you price match?

While we don't directly match prices, we're confident our quality and service provide the best value. We encourage you to compare not just price, but also products, techniques, and warranties.
