Interior Detailing FAQ

How long does interior detailing take?

Level 1: 1-2 hours. Level 2: 2-3 hours. Level 3: 3-4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

Do you remove all stains?

We have a high success rate with most stains. Some permanent stains may lighten but not completely disappear. We'll assess and advise during service.

Is the service mobile?

Yes! We come to your home or office with all equipment and supplies needed.

Do you clean child car seats?

Yes, we can clean child car seats in place or remove them for thorough cleaning if needed.
