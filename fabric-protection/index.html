Fabric Protection FAQs

Common questions about our protection services

How long does fabric protection last?

Professional application lasts 2+ years with normal use. Heavy-use vehicles may need reapplication sooner. We guarantee our protection for 2 full years.

Is it safe for kids and pets?

Absolutely! Once cured (about 1 hour), the protection is completely safe and non-toxic. It's odorless and won't affect those with allergies or sensitivities.

Can you protect leather seats?

Leather requires different protection products. We offer specialized leather protection and conditioning services that prevent cracking, fading, and staining.

Will it change the feel of my fabric?

No! Professional protection is invisible and doesn't change the texture, color, or feel of your fabric. You won't even know it's there until you spill something.

What if I already have stains?

We'll clean and treat existing stains before applying protection. Most stains can be removed or significantly improved. Protection prevents future staining.

How much does it cost?

Fabric protection starts at $149 for small vehicles and typically ranges $149-299 depending on vehicle size. It's often bundled with detailing for better value.

Do I need to reapply after cleaning?

No! Professional protection bonds to fibers and withstands normal cleaning. Only harsh chemicals or steam extraction may require touch-up application.

What's covered under warranty?

Our 2-year warranty covers the effectiveness of the protection. If staining occurs on properly maintained fabric, we'll retreat affected areas at no charge.
