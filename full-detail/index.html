Full Detail FAQs

Everything you need to know about our premium service

How long does a full detail take?

A proper full detail takes 6-8 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. We never rush - quality takes time. You can drop off or we'll work at your location.

Is full detail worth the cost?

Absolutely! You save over $200 vs booking services separately, plus get paint correction that's normally $300+ alone. The value and transformation are unmatched.

What's the difference from other details?

Full detail includes everything - paint correction, extraction cleaning, engine bay, ceramic protection. It's multiple services combined into one complete transformation.

How often should I get a full detail?

We recommend full detail 1-2 times per year, with maintenance washes between. Spring and fall are ideal times to protect against seasonal weather.

Can you remove all scratches?

Our paint correction removes 50-70% of swirls and light scratches. Deep scratches that catch your fingernail require separate paint repair services.

What's included in the warranty?

Our 6-month warranty covers the ceramic protection and any workmanship issues. If protection fails or you notice any issues, we'll fix it free.

Do you detail at my location?

Yes! We're fully mobile with water and power. We need shade for paint correction, so a garage is ideal but we can set up a canopy if needed.

Should I detail before selling?

Yes! Professional detailing typically returns 3-5x investment in sale price. Clean, shiny cars sell faster and for significantly more money.
