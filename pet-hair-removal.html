Pet Hair Removal FAQs

Common questions about our pet hair removal service

Can you really remove all pet hair?

We guarantee 99% removal of visible pet hair. Some microscopic dander may remain, but all visible hair will be eliminated using our specialized tools and techniques.

Will removal damage my car's fabric?

No. Our techniques are completely safe for all automotive fabrics including cloth, leather, and synthetic materials. We use appropriate methods for each surface type.

How long does pet hair removal take?

Quick removal takes 45-60 minutes as an add-on service. Complete standalone removal takes 2-3 hours depending on hair amount and vehicle size.

Do you also remove pet odors?

Yes! Basic odor treatment is included in all packages. For severe odors, we recommend our ozone treatment add-on for complete elimination.

What types of pet hair can you remove?

All types - dog, cat, rabbit, and other pet hair. We have specific techniques for different hair types from short smooth coats to long undercoats.

How often should I get pet hair removal?

For regular pet travelers, we recommend every 2-3 months. During heavy shedding seasons (spring/fall), monthly service may be beneficial.

Can you remove embedded hair from carpets?

Yes! Our air compression and rubber brush techniques are especially effective on carpet where hair becomes deeply embedded.

Is this service mobile?

Yes, we come to your location with all necessary equipment. We just need access to your vehicle - no power or water required from you.
