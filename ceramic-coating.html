Our Ceramic Coating Process

Professional application ensures maximum performance and longevity

1

Deep Decontamination Wash

We start with a thorough wash using pH-neutral soaps, followed by iron removal and clay bar treatment to remove all embedded contaminants from your paint surface.

2

Paint Correction

Using professional-grade polishers and compounds, we remove swirls, scratches, and oxidation. This ensures the coating bonds to a perfectly smooth surface for maximum gloss.

3

Surface Preparation

We use IPA (isopropyl alcohol) wipe-down to remove all polishing oils and residues, creating the ideal bonding surface for the ceramic coating.

4

Coating Application

Professional-grade ceramic coating is meticulously applied panel by panel in controlled conditions. Multiple layers are applied for premium packages, with proper curing time between each.

5

Curing & Inspection

The coating is allowed to cure properly (24-48 hours depending on package). We perform a final inspection under multiple light sources to ensure perfect, streak-free coverage.

6

Final Documentation

We register your warranty with CarFax, take final photos, and provide you with care instructions and your maintenance kit to keep your coating performing perfectly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ceramic coating

Is ceramic worth it in AZ heat?
Absolutely! Arizona's intense UV rays and 120¬∞+ surface temperatures can quickly damage unprotected paint. Ceramic coating provides essential UV resistance and heat protection.
Do you correct the paint first?
Yes, we include appropriate paint correction with every package. This removes swirls and imperfections, ensuring the coating bonds to a perfect surface for maximum gloss and protection.
Mobile or in-shop service?
Both! We offer mobile service throughout the East Valley with our fully-equipped units, or you can visit our Gilbert facility for controlled-environment application.
How long does it take?
Typically 4-8 hours depending on vehicle size and package selected. Elite packages may require keeping the vehicle overnight for proper curing.
Maintenance requirements?
Very minimal! Use pH-neutral car shampoo, avoid automatic car washes with brushes, and we recommend a maintenance detail every 3-6 months to keep the coating performing optimally.
Can I still wash my car normally?
Yes, but it's much easier! The hydrophobic properties mean dirt doesn't stick as much. Most dirt rinses off with just water, and drying is a breeze.
What's the difference between packages?
Layer count, durability, and included services. Essential is single-layer, Premium adds multiple layers plus glass, and Elite includes full vehicle protection with extended warranty.
Is the warranty real?
Yes! Our warranties are registered with CarFax and fully transferable. If the coating fails to perform as promised, we'll reapply it at no cost.
Can ceramic coating be removed?
Yes, but it requires machine polishing by a professional. The coating is designed to be permanent but can be removed if needed for paint work or reapplication.
Will it prevent all scratches?
Ceramic coating provides excellent protection against light scratches and swirl marks, but it won't prevent rock chips or deep scratches. For that level of protection, consider PPF.
Does it void my warranty?
No, ceramic coating is a protective layer that doesn't alter your vehicle's paint or components. It actually helps preserve your factory finish.
When can I wash after application?
We recommend waiting 7 days before the first wash to allow the coating to fully cure. After that, you can wash normally using the recommended products.
