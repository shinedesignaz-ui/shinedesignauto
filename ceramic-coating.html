Ceramic Coating FAQs

Get answers to common questions

How long does ceramic coating last?

Our ceramic coatings last 3-7+ years depending on the package selected, maintenance, and environmental factors. With proper care, many coatings exceed their warranty period.

Can ceramic coating be applied to new cars?

Absolutely! New cars are ideal candidates. Even new paint has minor imperfections from transport and dealer prep. We'll perfect and protect it from day one.

How long does installation take?

Installation typically takes 4-8 hours depending on vehicle size and package selected. We can complete most vehicles in a single day.

What maintenance is required?

Minimal maintenance! Just wash every 2-3 weeks with pH-neutral soap. We provide care instructions and recommend annual inspections to maintain warranty.

Will ceramic coating prevent all scratches?

Ceramic coating provides 9H hardness scratch resistance against light scratches and swirls. For rock chip protection, we recommend Paint Protection Film (PPF).

Is ceramic coating worth it in Arizona?

Absolutely! Arizona's intense UV rays and heat are brutal on paint. Ceramic coating provides essential UV protection and makes dust/monsoon cleanup much easier.

Can you apply coating in mobile service?

Yes! Our mobile units have controlled environments for proper application. We bring everything needed including power, lighting, and climate control.

What's included in the warranty?

Our warranties cover coating failure, excessive water spotting, and oxidation. They're manufacturer-backed, transferable, and documented on CarFax.
