Our Ceramic Coating Process

Meticulous preparation ensures perfect results

1

Initial Assessment

Inspect paint condition, measure thickness, identify defects. Discuss expectations and warranty options.

2

Deep Wash & Decontamination

Strip all waxes, iron fallout removal, clay bar treatment. Paint must be perfectly clean for bonding.

3

Paint Correction

Machine polish to remove swirls, scratches, and oxidation. Level of correction based on package.

4

Surface Preparation

IPA wipe down to remove all oils. Ensure perfect surface for ceramic bonding.

5

Ceramic Application

Apply ceramic coating in controlled environment. Multiple layers for premium packages.

6

Curing & Final Inspection

IR cure lamps accelerate bonding. Final inspection and documentation for warranty.

Ceramic Coating FAQs

Common questions about ceramic coating

How long does ceramic coating last?

Depending on the package and maintenance, ceramic coatings last 3-7+ years. Our lifetime options can last the life of your vehicle with proper care and annual inspections.

Can I wash my car after ceramic coating?

Yes! After the 7-day cure period, you can wash normally. In fact, washing becomes much easier - dirt slides off with just water. We recommend gentle hand washing for best results.

Does ceramic coating prevent all scratches?

Ceramic coating provides 9H hardness protection against light scratches and swirl marks. It won't prevent rock chips or deep scratches, but significantly reduces everyday wear. For maximum protection, combine with PPF.

Is paint correction necessary?

Yes, paint correction is crucial. Ceramic coating locks in the current condition, so we must perfect the paint first. All our packages include appropriate correction levels.

How long does installation take?

Installation typically takes 1-2 days depending on vehicle size and package. The vehicle then needs to cure for 24-48 hours before getting wet. Full cure takes 7 days.

What's the difference between packages?

Main differences are warranty length, number of coating layers, level of paint correction included, and which surfaces are protected. Higher packages include more surfaces and deeper correction.

Is ceramic coating worth it in Arizona?

Absolutely! Arizona's intense UV rays and heat are brutal on paint. Ceramic coating provides essential UV protection and makes washing easier despite our hard water. It's one of the best investments for our climate.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes! We offer maintenance washes and annual inspections to keep your coating performing optimally. Premium packages include free inspections and discounted maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ceramic coating scratch‑proof?

No coating is scratch‑proof, but quality ceramic coatings reduce micro‑marring and make wash‑induced scratches less likely when maintained properly.

How long does it last?

Our systems offer rated durability from 3 to 7+ years depending on product and care.

Do I need paint correction first?

We recommend at least a single‑stage polish to remove swirls so you lock in a near‑flawless finish under the coating.

How do I maintain it?

Use pH‑neutral soaps, quality towels, and periodic decontamination washes. Avoid abrasive polishing unless needed.
