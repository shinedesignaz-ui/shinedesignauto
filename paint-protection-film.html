Paint Protection Film FAQs

Common questions about PPF

How long does PPF last?

Quality PPF like XPEL and 3M comes with a 10-year warranty but often lasts 12-15 years with proper care. The film maintains its clarity and self-healing properties throughout its lifespan.

Can you see the film on the car?

When professionally installed, PPF is virtually invisible. The film is optically clear and the edges are carefully hidden or wrapped. Most people never notice it's there.

Does PPF damage paint when removed?

No, quality PPF is designed to be safely removable without damaging factory paint. Professional removal leaves your paint in the same condition it was when protected.

Can PPF be applied over ceramic coating?

Yes! Many clients choose PPF for high-impact areas and ceramic coating for the rest. We can also apply ceramic coating over PPF for ultimate protection.

How soon can I wash my car after installation?

We recommend waiting 48 hours before washing. After that, maintain as normal. Avoid pressure washing edges for the first week while the film fully cures.

What's covered under warranty?

Manufacturer warranties cover yellowing, cracking, peeling, staining, and delamination. Both XPEL and 3M offer comprehensive coverage that's transferable to new owners.

Is PPF worth it for daily drivers?

Absolutely! Daily drivers benefit most from PPF as they face constant exposure to road hazards. The film prevents costly paint repairs and maintains resale value.

Can you do custom coverage areas?

Yes! We can protect any area you're concerned about. Common additions include door handles, trunk ledges, rear bumper areas, and rocker panels.
