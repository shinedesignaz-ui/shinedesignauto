Motorcycle Detailing FAQs

Common questions about our bike detailing service

Do you detail all types of motorcycles?

Yes! We detail sport bikes, cruisers, touring bikes, adventure bikes, and even scooters. Each gets specialized care based on its unique needs.

How long does motorcycle detailing take?

Quick wash takes 30-45 minutes, Full Detail is about 90 minutes, and Show Prep requires 3 hours. We work efficiently while ensuring quality.

Can you remove scratches from my bike?

Minor scratches in paint and chrome can often be polished out. Deep scratches may require touch-up paint or professional repair, which we can advise on.

Do you offer ceramic coating for motorcycles?

Yes! Ceramic coating is included in our Show Prep package or available as an add-on. It provides long-lasting protection and makes cleaning easier.

Will you clean the chain?

Absolutely. Chain cleaning and lubrication is included in all packages. We use motorcycle-specific chain cleaners and lubricants.

Is it safe to wash my motorcycle?

Yes, when done properly. We avoid high-pressure water near bearings and electronics, and ensure everything is properly dried to prevent issues.

How often should I detail my motorcycle?

For regular riders, monthly quick washes with quarterly full details work well. Garage queens may only need detailing before rides or shows.

Do you work on vintage motorcycles?

Yes, and we take extra care with vintage bikes. We use gentle products and techniques appropriate for older finishes and materials.
