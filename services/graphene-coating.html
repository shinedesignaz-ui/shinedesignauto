FAQ

Is ceramic better than wax?

Yes—ceramics bond to paint, last years (not months), and offer superior chemical & UV resistance along with great water behavior.

How soon can I wash after coating?

Give the coating a few days to cure before the first wash. Avoid harsh chemicals and use coating‑safe soaps.

Will ceramic stop rock chips?

No—combine with PPF to prevent chips. Ceramic adds gloss and easy cleaning.
