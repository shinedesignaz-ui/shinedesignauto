Aircraft Detailing FAQ

What types of aircraft do you detail?

We detail all aircraft types from single-engine pistons to business jets. This includes Cessna, Piper, Beechcraft, Cirrus, Citation, Gulfstream, Learjet, helicopters, and more. Keegan has even detailed Boeing 737s and other commercial aircraft.

Do you come to our hangar?

Yes! We provide mobile service to your hangar, tie-down, or ramp location at any Arizona airport. We bring all necessary equipment and supplies.

Are your products aviation-approved?

Absolutely. We only use products specifically designed for aviation use that won't damage sensitive materials like plexiglass, aluminum, or composite structures.

How long does aircraft detailing take?

Timing varies by aircraft size and service level. A basic wash for a Cessna 172 takes 2-3 hours, while a complete detail for a business jet can take 8-12 hours. We work with your schedule.

Do you offer regular maintenance programs?

Yes! We offer monthly, quarterly, and custom maintenance programs for aircraft owners and flight schools. Regular detailing protects your investment and maintains value.

Can you work around our flight schedule?

Of course. We understand aviation schedules and offer flexible timing including early mornings, evenings, and weekends to minimize aircraft downtime.
