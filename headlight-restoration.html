Why Do Headlights Turn Yellow?

Understanding the problem helps prevent future damage

‚òÄÔ∏è

UV Damage

Arizona's intense sun breaks down the protective coating, causing the plastic to oxidize and turn yellow. This happens to all headlights over time.

üå°Ô∏è

Heat Exposure

Extreme temperatures cause the polycarbonate to expand and contract, creating micro-cracks that trap dirt and moisture, accelerating deterioration.

üí®

Road Debris

Sand, rocks, and road chemicals create tiny scratches and pitting. These imperfections scatter light, reducing brightness and clarity.

‚è∞

Age & Wear

Factory coatings typically last 3-5 years. Once worn away, oxidation accelerates rapidly, making headlights foggy and reducing light output.

Headlight Restoration FAQs

Common questions about our restoration service

How long does restoration last?

With proper UV sealant application, restoration typically lasts 1-2 years. Our premium service with ceramic coating can last 2-3 years or more.

Is it better than DIY kits?

Absolutely. DIY kits provide temporary results (3-6 months) and require significant effort. Professional restoration lasts years and includes warranty.

Can all headlights be restored?

Most can be restored to 80-95% clarity. Severely cracked or damaged headlights may need replacement. We'll assess and advise honestly.

Will it void my warranty?

No. Headlight restoration is considered maintenance, not modification. It won't affect your vehicle warranty.

How often should I restore?

Typically every 2-3 years depending on sun exposure and driving conditions. Regular washing and waxing can extend time between restorations.

Do you replace bulbs too?

Yes! We can replace dim or burnt bulbs during restoration. Premium LED upgrade bulbs are available for better visibility.

What's your warranty cover?

We guarantee against re-yellowing and cloudiness. If headlights yellow within warranty period, we'll re-restore them free.

Can you fix moisture inside?

We can often remove moisture and reseal headlights. If the housing is cracked, replacement may be necessary.
