Vinyl Wrap FAQs

Everything you need to know about vehicle wraps

How long does vinyl wrap last?

Quality vinyl wraps last 5-7 years with proper care. We use 3M and Avery Dennison materials with manufacturer warranties. Garage-kept vehicles can last even longer.

Will wrap damage my paint?

No! When installed and removed properly, vinyl wrap actually protects your paint. It shields from UV rays, minor scratches, and rock chips. Paint is preserved underneath.

Can wrapped cars go through car washes?

Yes, but hand washing is recommended. After 2 weeks of curing, touchless car washes are safe. Avoid brushes which can lift edges. We provide care instructions.

How much does a full wrap cost?

Full wraps start at $2,499 for sedans, $2,999 for SUVs, and $3,499 for trucks. Price varies by vehicle size, vinyl type, and complexity. We offer free quotes.

Can you wrap over dents or rust?

Vinyl conforms to the surface, so imperfections will show through. We recommend bodywork first for best results. Minor imperfections can sometimes be minimized with proper technique.

What's the installation time?

Full wraps take 3-5 days. Partial wraps 1-2 days. Chrome delete same day. We provide accurate timelines during consultation based on your specific project.

Do you offer financing?

Yes! We offer 6-24 month financing plans with approved credit. Many customers prefer financing to spread the cost while enjoying their new look immediately.

Can I design custom graphics?

Absolutely! We have in-house designers or can work with your artwork. Custom printed wraps let you create any design, pattern, or branding you imagine.
