1

Assessment & Preparation

Inspect paint condition, measure thickness, identify defects. Set up our mobile equipment.

Exterior Detailing FAQs

Answers to common questions about paint correction and protection

What is paint correction?

Paint correction is the process of removing surface imperfections like swirls, scratches, oxidation, and etching through machine polishing. We can remove 70-90% of defects.

How long does ceramic coating last?

Professional ceramic coatings last 1-5 years depending on the product and maintenance. They provide superior protection compared to traditional wax (3-6 months).

Can you remove all scratches?

We can remove most swirls and light scratches. Deep scratches that go through the clear coat may require touch-up paint. We'll assess and advise during inspection.

Is paint correction safe?

Yes, when done properly. We measure paint thickness and use the least aggressive method needed. Our certified technicians ensure safe, professional results.

How often should I detail my exterior?

Maintenance wash monthly, enhancement detail every 3-4 months, and paint correction as needed (typically every 1-2 years depending on condition).

Do you need shade to work?

Ideally yes, but we bring pop-up canopies if needed. Direct sun and high heat can affect product performance, so we plan accordingly.

What's included in ceramic coating?

Full paint decontamination, light polish to ensure bonding, professional coating application, and curing. Includes care instructions and maintenance products.

Can you detail wrapped vehicles?

Yes! We offer specialized care for vinyl wraps and PPF using safe products and techniques that won't damage or lift edges.
