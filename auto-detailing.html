Detailing FAQs

Common questions about our mobile detailing service

How long does detailing take?

Silver packages take 2-3 hours, Gold 3-4 hours, and Platinum 4-5 hours. We'll give you an accurate time when booking based on your vehicle's size and condition.

Do you need water and power access?

No! Our mobile units are completely self-sufficient. We bring our own water, power generator, and all equipment. You just provide the vehicle.

What areas do you service?

We cover Gilbert, Mesa, Chandler, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley, Scottsdale, Tempe, and Phoenix. Outside areas may have a small travel fee.

Can you remove pet hair?

Yes! We specialize in pet hair removal using special tools and techniques. It's included in our Platinum package or available as a $65 add-on.

Do you detail at offices?

Absolutely! Many clients have us detail while they work. We just need a parking space - we handle everything else.

What's the difference between detailing and a car wash?

Detailing is deep cleaning and protection using professional techniques and products. Car washes are surface-level only. We restore and protect, not just rinse.

How often should I detail my car?

For best results, full detail every 3-4 months with maintenance washes between. High-use vehicles or those parked outside may need more frequent service.

Is detailing worth it for older cars?

Absolutely! Detailing can transform older vehicles, making them look years newer. It's often the most cost-effective way to improve an older car's appearance and value.
