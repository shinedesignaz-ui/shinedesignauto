Types of Scratches We Fix

Identify your damage type for accurate pricing

✨

Clear Coat Scratches

Surface-level scratches that don't penetrate the paint. Most common from automatic car washes, bushes, or light contact.

● Mild Severity - $75-150
🎨

Paint Scratches

Deeper scratches through the color coat but not to primer. Visible white or different color lines. Common from keys or shopping carts.

● Moderate - $150-300
🔧

Deep Scratches

Through paint to primer or metal. May show gray primer or bare metal. Requires touch-up paint and professional blending.

● Severe - $300-500+
💥

Bumper Scuffs

Plastic bumper damage from parking. Often includes paint transfer from other vehicles. Most can be repaired without replacement.

● Varies - $150-400
🚗

Door Dings

Small dents with paint damage from car doors. Paintless dent repair combined with touch-up for seamless results.

● Per Panel - $200-350
🪟

Swirl Marks

Circular scratches from improper washing. Creates hazy, dull appearance. Fixed with machine polishing and correction.

● Full Car - $299-499

Scratch Repair FAQs

Common questions about scratch and paint repair

Can all scratches be fixed?

Most scratches can be repaired or significantly improved. Surface scratches buff out completely. Deep scratches to metal require touch-up but can be made nearly invisible with proper technique.

Will the repair match my paint?

Yes! We use factory paint codes and computerized matching to ensure perfect color match. We also blend the repair area into surrounding paint for seamless results.

How long does repair take?

Minor scratches: 1-2 hours. Multiple scratches: 2-4 hours. Deep damage or full panels: 1-2 days. We provide accurate time estimates during inspection.

Is it cheaper than body shop?

Yes, typically 50-70% less than traditional body shops. We repair only damaged areas rather than entire panels, saving you money while delivering equal quality.

Do you work with insurance?

Yes! We work with all major insurance companies, provide detailed estimates, and can bill insurance directly. We handle the paperwork for you.

How do I know if it's too deep?

Run your fingernail across the scratch. If it catches, it's through the clear coat. If you see white, gray, or metal, it's deep. Send us a photo for free assessment.

Will repairs last?

Yes! Professional repairs using quality materials last the life of your vehicle. We warranty our work against fading, peeling, or failure.

Can you fix rock chips?

Absolutely! Rock chips are filled, leveled, and painted to match. Small chips can be made nearly invisible. Larger chips may show slight texture but color will match perfectly.
